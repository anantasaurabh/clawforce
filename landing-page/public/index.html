Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ClawForce?

ClawForce is an autonomous AI agent orchestration platform. It deploys specialized AI agents that independently execute real business tasks — from managing social media accounts and generating content to conducting market research and automating outreach — all without the need for constant human supervision.

How do ClawForce AI agents work?

Each ClawForce agent is a specialized autonomous AI unit designed for a specific business function. You simply assign a task through the ClawForce HQ dashboard, and the agent executes it end-to-end: connecting to required services via secure OAuth, processing the task with real-time telemetry, and delivering results — all autonomously.

What tasks can ClawForce agents perform?

ClawForce agents can perform a wide range of business tasks including social media posting and scheduling, content creation and copywriting, market and competitor research, email outreach automation, data extraction and analysis, and more. New agent capabilities are continuously being added to the platform.

Is ClawForce free to use?

ClawForce is currently in early preview. You can request free access by signing up on this page. Early preview users will get first access to the platform and all its agent capabilities at no cost during the preview period.

Is my data secure with ClawForce?

Absolutely. ClawForce uses AES-256 encryption for all stored credentials, secure OAuth 2.0 for third-party service connections, and operates on isolated per-user data vaults in Google Cloud Firestore. Your API keys and tokens are never shared between users.

What platforms does ClawForce integrate with?

ClawForce integrates with major business platforms through secure OAuth 2.0 including LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and more. The platform is designed to be extensible — new integrations are added regularly based on community requests.
